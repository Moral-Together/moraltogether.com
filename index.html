Our Story

The organization was founded on a simple yet profound belief:
when good forces unite — a powerful ecosystem is created that is truly capable of impact.

Our goal is to serve as a connecting channel between organizations, nonprofits, initiatives, and groups — to create a space for sharing, synchronization, and collaboration, and to amplify the cumulative impact of social and community work.

We work to connect people and organizations acting for society, to make information accessible, to encourage collaborative dialogue, and to build an infrastructure of mutual recognition, trust, and joint action.

At our core is strengthening morale. We believe that positive morale is a driving force — for individuals, communities, and entire systems. That's why our perspective is always directed toward the good that exists.

"This is a home for a network of action. A place where initiatives meet, forces unite, and good resonates."

Our Vision

To serve as the organization of organized abundance for the people — to act as a connecting, positive, and empowering junction.

We aspire to create a living ecosystem of giving, partnership, and mutual solidarity, where resources, knowledge, opportunities, and goodwill flow to where they are truly needed.

Through smart connections, constructive communication, and an optimistic perspective on reality — we strengthen morale, highlight good deeds, and increase the sense of capability and hope in society. Our goal is to turn dispersed abundance into an organized force that works for the common good.

Our Activities

Our tools for creating a better reality

Digital Community

MoralForGood

The app enables every user to be part of a network of good: to offer help, request assistance, share initiatives, and publish activities.

MoralForGood was built on the understanding that when points of goodwill are connected — real community power is created. It centralizes information, connects complementary parties, and enables local and nationwide collaborations.

  • Real-time connection of needs to resources
  • Mapping of capabilities and needs, connecting complementary parties
  • A digital space that translates goodwill into action
Radio Channel

Radio M1

A radio channel broadcasting around the clock with positive content. Music that lifts the spirit and dialogue that connects people and cultures.

The place where music meets the soul and words create connection.
